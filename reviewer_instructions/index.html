Reviewer Instructions

As a reviewer, we will ask you to do the following:

  1. Summarize the paper in a few sentences.
  2. Indicate if the paper is on or off topic.
  3. Choose an overall evaluation score for the submission. The choices are: accept, borderline accept, borderline reject, and reject.
  4. Provide a justification for your overall evaluation score.
  5. List between 1 to 3 questions that you would ask the authors if you were to discuss this paper at a poster session or during another follow-up discussion.
  6. Rate your confidence in the evaluation of the submission.


Questions? Contact us at odej.kao@tu-berlin.de.
